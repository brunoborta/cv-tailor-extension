# CV Tailor — Chrome Web Store Description

## Short description (101 chars)

Generate AI-tailored CVs and cover letters for any job posting in seconds. Works with any AI provider.

## Long description

CV Tailor reads the job posting you're on and uses AI to generate a tailored CV and cover letter — matching keywords, rewriting bullets, and adapting your experience to the role.

Two modes:
• ATS Optimize — keeps your real experience and rewrites it to pass applicant tracking systems
• From Scratch — builds a complete CV from your profile when you're pivoting roles or industries

Works with any AI provider: Groq (free), Gemini (free), DeepSeek, OpenAI, Claude, or your own custom endpoint.

Your data never leaves your machine — everything is stored locally via Chrome's storage API. No backend, no account required.
